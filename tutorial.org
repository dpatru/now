** recording time

*** simple
$ now a simple task 

*** estimates 
$ now :10 a ten minute task 

*** hierarchy 
$ now :10 my project: ten minute task 

*** breaks 
$ now break 

*** text file
$ tail .now
<timestamp> a simple task 
<timestamp> :10 a ten minute task 
<timestamp> :10 my project: ten minute task 
<timestamp> break
